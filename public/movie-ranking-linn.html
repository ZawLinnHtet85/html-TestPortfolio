The Best TV Series according to Linn

My Top 3 TV Series that I watched in 2024

Mare of Easttown

One of the best series I have seen in awhile. A brilliant 7 part series. Kate Winslet was brilliant of course, her character was miserable but so likeable! Storyline was full of cliffhangers and there are a couple of fantastic twists!

The Day of the Jackal

This was the best cat and mouse, contract killer vs MI6 show I have ever seen. A mix of mission impossible and 007 with the best cast, direction, production and writing that really makes this tv series stand out and most likely the best of the year!

The Madness

Wrong place at the wrong time - some get out of this easily, some don’t and some just can’t - this series is the last case where the system just locks in and suffocates one person.
